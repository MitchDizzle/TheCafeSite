How Ordering Works

  1. Pick a package — or tell us what you have in mind; custom orders are welcome.
  2. Call or send the form — we'll talk through the date, headcount, and any dietary needs.
  3. We confirm the details — you'll get a firm quote and a confirmed date.
  4. Enjoy, then settle up — pay by check at delivery or pickup. No online payment needed.
