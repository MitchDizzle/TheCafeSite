# The Cafe Application

As a breakdown I think 3 different parts are required for this application process:

- **Web Application (And formal website)**
  - Cart system: Being able to pull in what a customer will want to order
  - Profile system: User login and other account display for recurring customers
  - Sessions: Storing information in case of refresh and other information
- **Employee/Admin Webpanel**
  - A webpanel that employees or admins can access customer information and other tools that can be used outside of the view of customers.
  - Includes the ability to change things on the menu and web pages through admin
- **Backend Server**
  - Processes the information and serves the web application pages and communication with the front end.

## User Stories

This contains stories which are made up situations that reflect what the user will want to be able to do. This could be broad or a specific situation.

Most of these stories are in the perspective of an 'Account' that provides groups permissions. Customer accounts and employee accounts are structurally different.

### Customer Stories

List of stories from the perspective of a customer visiting the application/site. From interacting with the pages to checking out with the required meal and account services.

As a customer,

- I want to be able to submit a request for catering services. A fill out form for initial contact.
- I want to pre-order a week's worth of designated meals that may be delivered or picked up at the cafe.

### Employee Stories

Stories from the perspective of an employee, someone who has escalated access to view, perhaps with different permissions, but not enough to remove or delete.

As an Employee,

- I should have access to bring up order information to be viewable.
