Catering Request Form

The Café Catering will provide as much or little help as you decide.  We can provide full sit down dinners or buffets with china, silverware, glassware and linen. Or we can provide plastic ware for your event. Whatever your budget is we can work with you.  We can also provide just the food if you like.  The catering form will help us better understand your event and needs. Please provide as much detail as possible and let us know if you have any special requirements. We are here to fill your catering needs.  We would love to hear from you! Please fill out this form and we will get in touch with you shortly.
