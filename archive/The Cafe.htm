Welcome to The Café. We are located in the Abernathy Building in Leavenworth, Kansas.

The Café located in the Abernathy Building down by the Centennial Bridge has been in business since October 2005.  We feature daily specials and all types of wraps, salads, burgers, chicken, hot & cold sandwiches. Come try us out!!

We are open every weekday for breakfast and lunch.  We also offer catering services.
